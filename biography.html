THE LEGACY

The Boy Who Conquered The World

STORY
1987 — 2000

The Boy from Rosario

Born in Rosario, Argentina, Lionel Andrés Messi developed a passion for football at an early age. Despite being diagnosed with a growth hormone deficiency, his undeniable talent caught the eye of FC Barcelona, who famously offered to pay for his medical treatments if he moved to Spain.

2000 — 2021

The King of Catalonia

Graduating from the famed La Masia academy, Messi made his competitive debut at age 17. Over the next two decades, he rewrote the history books. Winning 4 Champions League titles and shattering the all-time scoring record, he cemented himself as the greatest player to ever wear the Blaugrana shirt.

2021 — 2022

World Cup Glory

After years of heartbreak on the international stage, Messi led Argentina to glory in the 2021 Copa America. But his ultimate crowning achievement came at the 2022 FIFA World Cup in Qatar, where he delivered a mesmerizing performance to bring the trophy back to Argentina, completing football.

2023 — Present

The American Dream

Following a brief stint at PSG, Messi brought his talents to the United States, joining Inter Miami CF. His arrival instantly revolutionized Major League Soccer, bringing unprecedented global attention to the league and securing the Leagues Cup within weeks of his debut.
